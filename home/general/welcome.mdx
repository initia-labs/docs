# Welcome to Initia Docs
This is your gateway to understanding and building on Initia.
Whether you're a user exploring Initia's ecosystem or a developer seeking comprehensive guides,
you'll find everything you need here.

## Getting Started 
### Users
<Steps>
  <Step title="Set Up Your Wallet" icon="wallet">Don't have a wallet yet? Install the [Initia Wallet](/home/core-applications/initia-wallet) to start interacting with Initia</Step> 
  <Step title="Bridge Your Assets" icon="bridge">[Bridge](/home/core-applications/initia-bridge) your assets over from other chains to Initia</Step> 
  <Step title="View Your Account" icon="chart-simple">View your balances, transactions, and more on [InitiaScan](/home/core-applications/initia-scan)</Step> 
</Steps>

### Rollup & Node Operators
<Steps>
  <Step title="Deploy a Rollup" icon="cloud-arrow-up">Learn how to setup and [deploy your own rollup](/initia-and-minitias/deploying-minitias/minitia-deployment/introduction)</Step> 
  <Step title="Run a Node" icon="wrench">Easily spin up an [Initia node](/initia-and-minitias/running-a-node/introduction)</Step> 
  <Step title="Operate a Validator" icon="shield-check">[Become a validator](/initia-and-minitias/running-a-node/running-a-validator/becoming-a-validator) and help secure the network</Step> 
</Steps>
