## Overview

The **Interwoven Stack** is Initia's comprehensive, all-in-one solution designed to empower developers and teams in building and deploying rollups with unmatched ease and efficiency.

Historically, building a rollup requires developers to research, select, and piece together a multitude of components and services. These items range from

- choosing the right scaling solution, taking into account the VM compatibility for each option
- sourcing, or building your own wallets, explorers, bridges, and other essential tools.
- integrating various services, such as oracles, fiat on-ramps, and more.

All of these takes time and resources away from the developers to focus on building their application.

The Interwoven Stack aims to address these challenges through focusing on the following key areas:

1. **Comprehensive Out-of-the-Box Solution:** The Stack provides developers and teams with everything needed to launch and operate a rollup, from bridges, [wallets](/home/core-applications/initia-wallet), [explorers](/home/core-applications/initia-scan), to a multi-VM compatible [rollup framework](/home/core-concepts/interwoven-stack/opinit-framework).

2. **Seamless Interoperability:** The tools built into the Stack facilitate easy communication for the rollup, both between rollups and with Initia L1, creating a connected and cohesive user experience.

3. **Enhanced Security:** Robust security measures are baked into the stack, providing a solid foundation for building secure and trustworthy applications.

With this approach, developers can solely focus on building, improving, and shipping their application, while the Interwoven Stack handles the heavy lifting of selecting the right tools and components for a performant and secure rollup as well as a smooth, optimal user experience.

### Solutions

All rollups that leverages the Interwoven Stack comes with a large suite of pre-built, natively integrated tools and services.

- **Initia Product Suite:**
  - Initia App
  - Initia Wallet
  - Initia Usernames
  - Initia Scan

- **Financial Infrastructure:**
  - Native USDC through [Noble](https://nobleassets.xyz)
  - Fiat on-ramps via [Kado](https://kado.money)

- **Technical Infrastructure:**
  - [Skip](https://skip.money)'s [Block SDK](https://skip-protocol-docs.netlify.app/blocksdk/overview/) for programmable mempools and blocks
  - Connect native price oracle integration.

and more.
